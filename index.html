ABOUT ANGE MUKUNDENTE

Hello, I'm Ange Mukundente, a dedicated Backend Developer currently pursuing a B.Tech in Software Development at African Leadership University. I am committed to crafting robust and efficient web solutions.

My expertise spans HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React.js, Node.js, with a strong focus on responsive design. I firmly believe in the principle that exceptional design goes beyond aesthetics. It involves creating websites that not only captivate the eye but also prioritize user-friendliness and universal accessibility.
